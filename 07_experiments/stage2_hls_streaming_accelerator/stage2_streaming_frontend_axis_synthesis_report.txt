## Current interpretation

The AXI4-Stream interface is now syntactically and functionally verified,
and the design meets the 5 ns estimated timing target. However, the current
wrapper still materializes the full input and output feature maps in local
buffers, consuming 256 of the 280 available BRAM_18K blocks. It is therefore
an AXI interface baseline rather than the final streaming accelerator.

The next hardware optimization is to remove both full-size staging arrays
and connect the AXI stream directly to a line-buffer/window-buffer dataflow.
